Irrigation Association

The Irrigation Association plays a vital role in managing and maintaining the water distribution systems for agricultural purposes. This initiative is part of the National Irrigation Administration's efforts to ensure sustainable and efficient water usage.

  • Promoting equitable water distribution among farmers
  • Providing training and support for irrigation management
  • Collaborating with local communities for system improvements
  • Ensuring the maintenance and repair of irrigation infrastructure

For more information or to get involved, please contact us at irrigation@nia.gov.
